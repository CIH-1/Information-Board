## **1. CAC Registration Update**

Ope informed the members that the organisation’s CAC registration has been approved, and the organisation’s name has been officially changed to **ChariCare Impact Foundation**.
He further explained that the newsletter publication is the current stage of the registration process, and progress is ongoing.

---

## **2. Organisational Structure and Governance**

Ini discussed the governing structure of the organisation, which is divided into three (3) bodies:

* Board of Trustees
* Core Operational Management
* Programmatic and Execution Structure

The team confirmed that discussions are currently ongoing with selected individuals to serve as members of the Board of Trustees.

---

## **5. Financial Updates and Grants**

The organisation won a grant of **€1,500**, to be disbursed in two instalments.
The first payment is expected between January and April 2026.

Ope also shared information about another grant opportunity with a deadline of December 31, 2025.

### **CAC Registration Fees**

* Initial registration fee: ₦180,000
* Discount received: ₦40,000
* Revised amount: ₦140,000
* Amount paid so far: ₦100,000

Due to delays, CAC increased the fee to double the price, requiring an additional ₦60,000.
The organisation currently has ₦50,000, leaving a shortfall to be addressed.

## **6. Administrative and Branding Matters**

Lateefah read the story of how ChariCare Impact Foundation was formed.

The team discussed organisational merchandise, including branded tops and caps, which were agreed to be essential for visibility and outreach activities.
